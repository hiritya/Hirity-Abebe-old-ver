About Me

Greetings! I'm Hirity Abebe, a passionate digital designer dedicated to crafting engaging and user-friendly digital experiences. My journey in the realm of digital design began during my undergraduate study in Studio Art and Technology at Purdue University. I dove further into graphic design in my position as a web and multimedia content developer intern at Indiana University, and now work there as a Part-Time Graphic Designer. This invaluable experience allowed me to hone my skills and gain practical insights into the dynamic world multimedia integration and visual storytelling.

Recently, I graduated with a certificate in Information Technology with a focus on web development. I am committed to staying at the forefront of industry trends and adopting cutting-edge technologies. My goal is to seamlessly blend creativity with technical proficiency to bring ideas to life in the digital landscape.

With a solid foundation in graphic design and web development, coupled with a relentless pursuit of knowledge, I am poised to contribute effectively to innovative and impactful projects. Let's collaborate and create something extraordinary together!
