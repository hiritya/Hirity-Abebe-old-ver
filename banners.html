Homepage Banners

Homepage Banners made for Indiana University Courses

Project Overview

When creating homepage banners, I am tasked with enhancing the visual appeal of Indiana University College Courses by creating vibrant and engaging banners. I utilize Adobe Illustrator's vector-based design tools to bring these banners to life. Leveraging my proficiency in graphic design principles, I designed each banner to blend aesthetic design with informational clarity. Adobe Photoshop applied when refining the visual elements, allowing for a polished and professional finish.

The toolkit for these projects were Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, with the incorporation of Adobe Stock. This resource enriched the design process by providing access to a vast repository of high-quality images and visual elements, facilitating the creation of visually stunning and contextually relevant banners.

Collaboration is the most important step, as I worked closely with Instructional Designers who collaborate with professors to shape the educational content. Working with them has allowed me to create graphics that capture the principles of the courses but also resonated with the instructional objectives. Collaborating with the Instructional Designers gave me valuable insights into the course content. This collaborative approach fostered a dynamic and iterative design process, resulting in banners that served as impactful visual assets for the respective Indiana University College Courses.

Tools used

Adobe Illustrator Adobe Photoshop Adobe Stock Adobe Color Wheel
